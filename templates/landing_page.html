Find All the Violators at One Place

The violators originated from the video can be located at one place with their details such as vehicle number, location, date and time of violation, category of the violated vehicle etc. This is important as all the violators from many videos can collectively be found at one location for the easy access and further investigation.

Upload the videos with Ease

Videos to find the violators of bus lane can be uploaded through a friendly interface that makes the process easy and hassle free for the police officials. The process consists of 3 simple steps: Add the location, Select the video file, Click the Upload button and done!

Keep track of all the uploaded videos

The uploaded videos which capture the violators of the bus lane can be tracked at one place with details such as the uploaded date and time, the progress of completion and so on. This helps in avoiding redundancy and knowing status of the uploading videos.

No waiting after Uploading

When a video is uploaded, certain time is required for the processing of the video. But the user does not have to wait this entire processing time. Instead, they can navigate throughout the application and complete other tasks such as searching, uploading another video, etc until the results are fetched from the video. This saves time for the user and allows them to complete other task in the processing time.
